### QA-066 — Cultural Values: Economic & Demographic Effects

**Status:** ✅ Implemented

**Problem:** Culture traits were purely narrative. An "Isolationist" world generated the same trade volume and ship counts as a "Cosmopolitan" one. A "Gender Superiority" culture had the same workforce participation as an egalitarian one.

**Solution:** Added a `CULTURAL_MECHANICAL_EFFECTS` map that assigns quantitative deltas to each culture trait, then applied them during inhabitant generation.

**Mechanical effects implemented:**

| Effect | Description | Example Traits |
|--------|-------------|----------------|
| `wealthDelta` | Modifier to the 2D6 wealth roll | Greed is Good (+2), Money is Evil (−2) |
| `developmentDelta` | Modifier to the 2D6 development roll | Meritocratic Examination (+2), Disability Discrimination (−1) |
| `tradeMultiplier` | Multiplier to ships-in-area trade budget | Xenophobia (×0.5), Cultural Exchange (×1.4), Xenocide (×0) |
| `workforceMultiplier` | Multiplier to effective population | Gender Superiority (×0.5) |
| `travelZoneDelta` | Shift toward Amber/Red zone | Isolationist (+1), Xenophilia (−1) |

**Files changed:**

1. **`src/types/inhabitants.ts`**: Added `effectivePopulation`, `tradeMultiplier`, `culturalEffectsBreakdown` to `Inhabitants`.
2. **`src/lib/worldDataCulture.ts`**: Added `CulturalMechanicalEffect` interface, `CULTURAL_MECHANICAL_EFFECTS` map (~30 traits), and `applyCulturalEffects()` helper.
3. **`src/lib/generatorInhabitants.ts`**: Restructured to generate culture traits BEFORE wealth/development rolls so deltas apply. Effective population now used for starport calculation.
4. **`src/lib/economicsEngine.ts`**: `recalculateStarportFromDials` now uses `effectivePopulation` when present.
5. **`src/lib/shipsInArea.ts`**: `generateShipsInTheArea` accepts optional `tradeMultiplier` parameter.
6. **`src/hooks/useInhabitantsActions.ts`**: Passes `inhabitants.tradeMultiplier` to ship generation.
7. **`src/components/tabs/InhabitantsTab.tsx`**: New "Cultural Economic Effects" panel shows effective population, trade multiplier, and per-trait breakdown.

**Design decisions:**
- Unknown traits produce no effect — the map can be extended incrementally.
- Trade multiplier floors at 0 (Xenocide → zero external trade).
- Workforce multiplier floors at 0.1 (never completely zeroes out a world).
- Wealth/development deltas are applied to the raw 2D6 roll before table lookup, with clamping to [2, 12].
- Travel zone delta shifts the zone along Green → Amber → Red.
